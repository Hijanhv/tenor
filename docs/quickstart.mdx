---
title: "Quickstart"
description: "Lock your first fixed rate on Stellar testnet in a couple of minutes."
---

Tenor runs on **Stellar testnet**. Test assets have no real value, so you can try
everything without risk.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install Freighter">
    Install the [Freighter wallet](https://www.freighter.app/) and switch it to the
    Test network.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the app and connect">
    Head to the [Tenor app](https://tenor-blond-xi.vercel.app/app) and connect your
    wallet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grab test tokens">
    Use the built in faucet to mint test USDC and TSY.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Lock a rate">
    Buy principal to lock a fixed rate, split TSY into principal and yield to trade
    the yield, or deposit into the carry vault.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Card title="Open the app" icon="rocket" href="https://tenor-blond-xi.vercel.app/app">
  Connect Freighter and lock your first fixed rate.
</Card>
