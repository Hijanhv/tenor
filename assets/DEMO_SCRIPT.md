# Tenor pitch and demo video script (about 75 seconds)

Keep it short. One clear promise, one live demo, one ask. Screen recording of the app
with a voiceover. Text in **bold** is what you say, text in _italics_ is what you show.

---

**The pitch (0:00 to 0:20)**

**"Stellar has real yield now, from Blend, DeFindex, tokenized treasuries. But every rate floats. You never know what next week pays, so you can't plan, and big money won't touch it."**

_Landing page hero on screen, the live fixed rate ticking._

**"Tenor is the fixed rate market Stellar was missing. We turn floating yield into a rate you can lock, or trade."**

---

**How it works (0:20 to 0:35)**

_Scroll to the PT and YT cards._

**"We split any yield asset into two tokens. PT, the principal, redeems for one dollar at maturity. YT collects the yield. Buy PT at a discount and the discount is your locked, guaranteed return."**

---

**The live demo (0:35 to 1:05)**

_Open the app, connect Freighter (testnet), click the faucet._

**"Here it is live on testnet. I connect my wallet and grab some test dollars."**

_Type an amount in Earn a fixed rate. The preview fills in._

**"I want a fixed rate. I type one hundred dollars, and it shows exactly what I lock: the principal I receive, the gain at maturity, and the rate, around nine and a half percent, read straight from the contract."**

_Click Lock fixed rate, approve in Freighter, the confirmation banner appears._

**"One signature. Done. That rate is now fixed on chain, no matter what floating rates do."**

_Point at the carry vault card._

**"Or one deposit into the carry vault and the strategy runs itself."**

---

**The ask (1:05 to 1:15)**

_Show the on chain panel with the contract links, then the GitHub and X handle._

**"It is all open source, nine of nine tests passing, and deployed today. Tenor, the fixed rate market for Stellar. Try it, and follow @tenor_stellar."**
